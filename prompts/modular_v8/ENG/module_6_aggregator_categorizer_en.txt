### STEP 1. DETERMINE CATEGORY (A/B/C/D)

Based on the article content (abstract + introduction + conclusions), select one category:

| Category | Type of work | Key markers |
|----------|--------------|--------------|
| A | Fundamental work | new theory, law, conceptual discovery, paradigm shift, creation of a new scientific direction |
| B | Applied research | experiment with active intervention, changing conditions, hypothesis testing on data, comparison of methods |
| C | Review-analytical | analysis, measurement, synthesis without intervention, systematic review, classification, description of a phenomenon |
| D | Scientific report / brief communication | short note, preliminary results, technical description without detailed methodology |

**Rule:** If an article falls under multiple categories > choose the higher one on the scale (A > B > C > D)

---

### STEP 2. FINAL SCORE CALCULATION (Execute silently, without output)

**Final Score = Novelty (0–30) + Methodology (0–25) + Practical Value (0–20) + Visualization (0–15) + Reproducibility (0–10)**

Range: 0–100
Execute silently, without output

**INTERPRETATION OF FINAL SCORE**

| Final Score | Recommendation |
|-------------|----------------|
| < 50 | Reject |
| 50–70 | Accept after revision |
| 70–90 | Accept with minor recommendations |
| 90–100 | Accept (highest standard) |
